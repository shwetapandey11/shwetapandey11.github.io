Zumba

Is a fitness program created by Colombian dancer and choreographer Alberto Beto. Zumba involves dance and aerobic elements. It also incorporates hip-hop soca, samba, salsa, merengue, mambo and martial arts. Squats and lunges are also included.

There are different types of classes for different levels of age and exertion.

  • Zumba Gold mainly targets the older population..
  • Zumba Toning is for the people who do their workouts with toning sticks.
  • Aqua Zumba is Zumba in a swimming pool.
  • Zumba in the circuit is the dance combined with circuit training.
  • Zumbatomic is designed for children between the ages of 4 and 12.
  • Zumba Gold-Toning is a toning class for older participants.
  • Zumba Sentao is a chair workout.

DO's
  • Have fun at all times! Smile and laugh!
  • Keep moving! Even if you can't follow some of the steps, keep moving!
  • Remember, no one is looking at you.So, no need to be self-conscious.
  • Wear comfortable shoes. Trainers are probably best.
  • Do take the class at your own pace until you feel comfortable enough to take it up a notch.

DONT’s
  • Give up!  Don't ever give up!  The more you come to class, the easier the moves get
  • Worry about what people think of you in class!
  • Feel the need to keep up with the instructor.  
  • Perform the Same Workout Everyday
  • Overdo It As You Start Your Program
  • Go From 0 to 60 in 5 Seconds
  • Stop Immediately After a Vigorous Workout
DIET
Foods rich in complex carbohydrates and lean proteins are a good choice in a Pilates diet as they will provide you with the energy necessary to perform your Pilates routine. It is also essential to keep the body hydrated while working out.

Weight loss and fat loss become more effective by mixing the intense Zumba workout with the sensible Zumba diet.

  • Drink as much water as you like. Add other ultra-low-calorie extras (lemon juice, vinegar, Splenda) as desired.
  • Eat real food; vegetables, fruit, free-range beef, poultry, wild fish.
    Focus on eating smaller portions during your meals.
  • The following are sources of protein on your Zumba diet:
    Protein Shakes, Smoothies adding whey protein egg whites & Organic Dairy.

So you have an idea on how to map out your Zumba diet and turbo charge your Zumba lifestyle.
