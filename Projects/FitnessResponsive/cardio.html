DO's

  • Do Pace Yourself Based on How You Feel
  • Be Choosey About Your Equipment
  • Cool Down Gradually
  • Set Appropriate Goals
  • Increase Exercise Level Gradually
  • Perform a Warm-Up Before Your Workout
  • Exercise Regularly Throughout the Week

DONT’s

  • Don't Be a Weekend Warrior
  • Get Hung Up on Heart Rate
  • Skip Your Warm-up
  • Perform the Same Workout Everyday
  • Overdo It As You Start Your Program
  • Go From 0 to 60 in 5 Seconds
  • Stop Immediately After a Vigorous Workout

DIET

Foods to Eat When Following a Yogic Diet :
  • Fruits, vegetables, except ginger & garlic
  • Whole grains, especially oats, wheat, and rice
  • Beans, tofu, mung, aduki & unsalted nuts and seeds
  • Herbal tea, raw sugar, sweet spices like cinnamon, cardamom, mint, basil, turmeric, ginger, cumin, fennel

Foods to Avoid or Reduce When Following a Yogic Diet
  • Meat and fish of all types, including eggs
  • Processed and/or artificial foods
  • Animal fats, margarine, fried foods
  • White flour, white sugar, garlic, onions, spicey foods
  • Alcohol, tobacco, stimulants
